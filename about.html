The Fearless Fowls

Chickens may seem like ordinary barnyard animals, but some of them have an attitude that rivals the toughest creatures in the wild. From chasing away intruders to standing their ground against predators, these fearless fowls refuse to back down.

In history, there have been tales of heroic chickens saving their flocks, escaping from danger, and even forming unexpected friendships with other animals. They are smarter than you think, and their courage knows no bounds.

One of the most famous badass chickens is Mike the Headless Chicken, who survived for 18 months after losing his head. This bizarre but true story proves just how resilient these birds can be.
